# Tenaz CRM Brand Assets

## Core

- `tenaz-symbol.svg`: primary standalone symbol.
- `tenaz-logo-horizontal.svg`: main horizontal lockup.
- `tenaz-logo-reverse.svg`: white lockup for dark backgrounds.
- `tenaz-app-icon.svg`: secondary digital app icon treatment.
- `tenaz-pattern.svg`: visual-system pattern for covers, decks, and interface accents.
- `tenaz-logo-board.svg`: reference board matching the approved asset layout.

## Palette

- Near black: `#07090F`
- Deep navy: `#0B1F3D`
- Accent blue: `#1B6CFF`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft reverse text: `#D9E6FF`

## Usage

Use the flat symbol or horizontal logo as the official mark. Use the app icon only for digital surfaces where depth is expected, such as app launchers or splash screens. Keep the central negative space subtle; it should suggest control and process timing by optical illusion, not become an explicit hourglass.
